# Investor Magnet Swarm

Investor magnet swarms position HackersEra as the category-defining automotive cybersecurity company through narrative, PR, LinkedIn, analyst relations, events, social proof, crisis response, and funding-round orchestration.

Default domain: cybersecurity and cybersecurity-testing solutions, especially automotive, embedded, hardware-assisted, and compliance-driven B2B products.

| Swarm | Agent | Responsibility |
|-------|-------|----------------|

| Funding Round Simulation | FundingRoundSimulationAgent | VC persona simulation, skeptical questions, rebuttal matrix, fundraising asset plan |

| Investor Metrics | InvestorMetricsAgent | market, traction, efficiency, product, team, moat, and buzz metrics |

## Execution Patterns

### Funding Round Simulation

Use before or during a seed, Series A/B, strategic investment, or acquisition conversation.

1. InvestorNarrativeAgent creates market, traction, moat, team, and category narratives.
2. FundingRoundSimulationAgent generates skeptical VC and strategic investor personas.
3. InvestorMetricsAgent checks numbers and evidence freshness.
4. SocialProofValidationAgent checks logo, partner, advisor, and testimonial permissions.
5. CEOAgent or FinanceInvestorRelationsSwarmAgent arbitrates the final story.
6. Output teaser deck, full deck, executive summary, video script, investor update, and LinkedIn announcement variants.

### News Cycle Orchestration

Use for funding announcements, product launches, major partnerships, standards milestones, threat research, or category reports.

1. PRMediaEngineAgent sequences tease, launch, sustain, and convert motions.
2. LinkedInThoughtLeadershipAgent coordinates founder, CTO, product, engineering, customer, and advisor posts.
3. AnalystRelationsAgent prepares category validation and briefing language.
4. SocialProofValidationAgent confirms permissions.
5. InvestorNarrativeAgent maps coverage into fundraising follow-ups.

### LinkedIn Category Leadership

Use for ongoing founder-led and technical-team-led market creation.

- Monday: CEO market trend essay and carousel.
- Tuesday: CTO technical deep dive or architecture thread.
- Wednesday: product/customer win with permission-safe proof.
- Thursday: engineering culture and hiring magnet story.
- Friday: industry news with HackersEra perspective.
- Saturday: founder reflection and long-term vision.
- Sunday: targeted comments, partner amplification, and community engagement.

### Analyst And Event Flywheel

Use to create enterprise and investor credibility.

- AnalystRelationsAgent schedules briefings and creates category language.
- EventConferenceAgent secures CFPs, demos, dinners, and awards.
- PRMediaEngineAgent turns briefings and events into media moments.
- InvestorNarrativeAgent adds proof to investor updates and decks.

### Crisis-To-Opportunity

Use when CVEs, recalls, breaches, regulatory fines, or market incidents occur.

1. CrisisOpportunityAgent separates public facts from speculation.
2. Technical/security swarms provide expert analysis.
3. PRMediaEngineAgent offers credible commentary.
4. LinkedInThoughtLeadershipAgent publishes calm market education.
5. InvestorNarrativeAgent creates a private "market validation" memo.

## Completion Standard

An investor magnet swarm is complete only when:

- target investor type and round context are explicit
- public and private narratives are separated
- all claims have evidence or are flagged as gaps
- media, LinkedIn, analyst, event, and social-proof motions reinforce one category story
- investor metrics and data-room readiness are checked
- external-facing content has an approval gate
