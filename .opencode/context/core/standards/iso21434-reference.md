# ISO/SAE 21434:2021 Reference — Road Vehicles Cybersecurity Engineering

**Purpose**: Provide OpenAgent and subagents with direct access to the complete ISO/SAE 21434:2021 standard repository for cybersecurity engineering, TARA, compliance evidence, and audit readiness.

**Location**: `@iso21434_standard/` (project root)

**Format**: 88 high-resolution JPG scans of all standard clauses, annexes, tables, figures, and work products. Agents with vision capabilities can read these images directly.

---

## Standard Overview

ISO/SAE 21434:2021 specifies requirements for cybersecurity risk management regarding engineering of electrical and electronic (E/E) systems in road vehicles, including their components and interfaces. It defines a structured framework for:

- Organizational cybersecurity management (Clause 5)
- Project-dependent cybersecurity management (Clause 6)
- Distributed cybersecurity activities (Clause 7)
- Continual cybersecurity activities (Clause 8)
- Concept phase (Clause 9)
- Product development (Clause 10)
- Cybersecurity validation (Clause 11)
- Production (Clause 12)
- Operations and maintenance (Clause 13)
- End of cybersecurity support and decommissioning (Clause 14)
- TARA methods (Clause 15)
