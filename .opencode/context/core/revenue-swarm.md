# Revenue Swarm

Revenue swarms behave like a self-directed growth organization. They discover markets, create demand, convert pipeline, retain customers, and improve strategy through adversarial multi-perspective iteration.

For this workspace and the user's product direction, default revenue work to cybersecurity and cybersecurity-testing solutions unless explicitly told otherwise.

## Execution Patterns

### Parallel Campaign Genesis

Use for market launches, new segments, new cybersecurity products, and event-led campaigns.

1. MarketIntelligenceAgent maps competitors, pricing, messaging, and triggers.
2. CustomerResearchAgent builds ICP, personas, JTBD, and objections.
3. BrandStrategyAgent creates positioning and proof hierarchy.
4. ContentSwarmAgent creates parallel asset angles.
5. ConversionAgent designs landing pages and A/B tests.
6. PRCommunicationsAgent pitches media/events with tailored angles.
7. ChiefGrowthOfficerAgent approves the campaign system and KPIs.

### Adversarial Sales Coaching

Use for enterprise deals, procurement pressure, technical objections, and competitive displacement.

1. SalesCoachAgent simulates skeptical buyer, procurement, technical evaluator, and competitor pressure.
2. BrandStrategyAgent checks message discipline.
3. PricingStrategyAgent tests TCO and discount pressure.
4. ChiefGrowthOfficerAgent extracts repeatable playbook improvements.

### Trust & Reputation Defense

Use for reviews, testimonials, public criticism, analyst-style proof, and community trust.

1. TrustReputationAgent monitors and classifies reputation signals.
2. PRCommunicationsAgent drafts response paths.
3. CustomerSuccessAgent identifies advocates.
4. ContentSwarmAgent turns proof into case studies and social assets.

### Investor Momentum

Use when GTM activity must support fundraising, strategic investors, analyst credibility, or founder-led category creation.

1. InvestorNarrativeAgent aligns campaign proof to the funding story.
2. PRMediaEngineAgent maps media coverage to investor social proof.
3. LinkedInThoughtLeadershipAgent creates founder, CTO, and technical team visibility.
4. InvestorMetricsAgent checks traction, buzz, and moat metrics before external use.

### Dynamic Pricing & Packaging

Use for pilot pricing, production conversion, renewals, upsell, and packaging strategy.

1. PricingStrategyAgent models packages and willingness-to-pay.
2. MarketIntelligenceAgent tracks competitor pricing and category norms.
3. PredictiveRevenueAgent flags churn/expansion risk.
4. ChiefGrowthOfficerAgent approves rules and experiments.

## Cybersecurity GTM Defaults

For HackersEra-style cybersecurity products:

- center trust, technical proof, compliance, and risk reduction
- speak separately to CISO, engineering, compliance, procurement, and practitioner personas
- use technical assets like attack-surface maps, maturity assessments, protocol/security guides, benchmark reports, and executive risk briefs
- avoid unsupported claims about certifications, customer logos, exploit capability, or compliance outcomes
- prefer visual technical proposals, battlecards, security maturity assessments, and pilot-to-production ladders

## Completion Standard

A revenue swarm is complete only when:

- target segment and personas are explicit
- claims are evidence-gated
- campaign assets map to funnel stage and persona
- success metrics and experiments are defined
- sales objections and rebuttals are captured
- trust/reputation paths are included for cybersecurity credibility
