# Operating Swarm

Use OpenAgent as the user-facing entrypoint. Route the request to the smallest useful business operating swarm.

## Instructions

1. Classify the operating domain:
   - support/success
   - product strategy
   - regulatory/compliance
   - talent/hiring
   - finance/investor relations
   - supply chain/manufacturing
   - innovation/R&D
   - crisis response
   - partnerships/ecosystem
   - knowledge management
2. Select the relevant swarm agent(s).
3. Include CEOAgent when signals cross business functions or require resource tradeoffs.
4. Produce artifacts, metrics, owners, and escalation paths.
5. Ask for approval before creating external-facing material or operational changes.
