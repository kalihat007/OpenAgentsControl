# Sales Coach

Run an adversarial sales simulation:

1. Define buyer personas and deal stage.
2. Simulate skeptical objections from CISO, engineering, procurement, compliance, and end-user roles.
3. Inject competitor pressure and TCO objections.
4. Score pitch, proof, objection handling, next-step control, and risk.
5. Produce rebuttals, talk tracks, and coaching drills.

For cybersecurity products, require evidence for risk-reduction, compliance, security methodology, and technical differentiation claims.
