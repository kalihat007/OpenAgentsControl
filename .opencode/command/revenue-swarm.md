# Revenue Swarm

Use OpenAgent as the user-facing entrypoint. Route this work to the revenue swarm roles needed for the request.

## Instructions

1. Classify the revenue motion:
   - campaign genesis
   - sales coaching
   - trust/reputation
   - pricing/packaging
   - retention/expansion
   - analytics/forecasting
2. Select the smallest useful role set.
3. For cybersecurity products, center trust, technical proof, compliance, and buyer risk reduction.
4. Produce a revenue-swarm plan with KPIs, roles, artifacts, experiments, and review gates.
5. Ask for approval before generating campaign assets or outreach.
